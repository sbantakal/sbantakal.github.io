About Me

"I am passionate developer, but more importantly I'm passionate about technology"

Hey, I'm Shashank

I am a full-stack developer, environmentalist, soccer enthusiast, amateur photographer, artist & an aspiring writer.

As a little fellow, I was always intrigued by the inner workings of toy cars & other gadgets; and would try to take them apart and try to put them back, much to the annoyance of my parents. Driven by this curiosity, I decided to pursue my bachelors in the field of electronics and communication engineering, graduating in the year 2015.

As a fledgling engineer, I soon found my true passion in programming. As a developer, I dealt with a lot of clunky, intuitive programs and softwares. Fascination lead to the decision to teach myself how to code.
Soon, I realized that I was enjoying what I was doing, the notion of using creativity & logic to solve problems with few lines of code drives me, and it helps that there is always more to learn.

I am a music enthusiast, currently teaching myself to play the guitar. In my leisure time i like to read, play soccer and video games.
